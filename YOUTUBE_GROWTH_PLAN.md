## 3. Strategy

1. **Double down on FOOD spectacle** (meat, village festivals, cooking, breakfast, markets).
   Proven organic magnet — every one brings in new genuine viewers for free.
2. **Use the renovation saga as "glue", not bait.** Low cold-start traction but highly bingeable.
   Chain it in playlists *after* a food video pulls people in.
3. **Stop all paid promotion.** The data shows it converted to neither engagement nor hours, and
   may have flagged the channel.
4. **Convert Shorts reach into long-form hours.** Shorts views (~2M) count ~0 toward YPP. End every
   Short with a CTA + description link to the matching long-form video.
5. **Playlists = #1 hours multiplier.** Build 3–4 bingeable playlists; autoplay chains one counted
   video into the next.
6. **Retention basics on every long-form:** hook in first 30s, pattern-interrupt every 30–60s,
   captions on, ≥8–10 min length, end screens to next episode in playlist.

## 5. 30-day publishing calendar (food-led)

Cadence: **2–3 long-form/week + daily Shorts as funnels.** Each week leads with a food video
(discovery), follows with a renovation/life episode (retention), and uses Shorts to feed both.

### Week 1 — Food hook
- **Mon (long-form):** "Sicilian Street Food Tour — what locals actually eat"
- **Wed (long-form):** Renovation episode — chain from Mon's video via end screen
- **Fri (long-form):** "We Cooked a Full Sicilian Feast with our Neighbours"
- **Daily Shorts:** best 30–45s moments from the above, each linking to its full video

### Week 2 — Village life + market
- **Mon:** "Inside a Sicilian Village Market — €X full haul"
- **Wed:** Renovation/house progress episode
- **Fri:** "Cooking [signature dish] the Sicilian Way" (recipe format)
- **Shorts:** the tasting reaction, the haul reveal, one recipe step

### Week 3 — Festival / event spectacle
- **Mon:** "We Joined a Sicilian Village Festival 🎉" (your highest-engagement format)
- **Wed:** Renovation milestone episode
- **Fri:** "Sicilian Breakfast & Morning Routine" (proven topic — refresh it)
- **Shorts:** festival highlights, food close-ups

### Week 4 — Evergreen + payoff
- **Mon:** Evergreen searchable: "What Nobody Tells You About Buying a House in Sicily"
- **Wed:** "Cost of Renovating an Italian House — real numbers"
- **Fri:** "A Day in Our Sicilian Life" (lifestyle, ties food + house together)
- **Shorts:** quick tips pulled from the evergreen videos
