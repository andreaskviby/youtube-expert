Published: October 30, 2025

Everything Went Wrong in Sicily. Drinks Overboard, Flying Window & Ceiling Disaster. What was supposed to be a calm, simple day in Sicily turned into pure chaos… in the funniest way possible. 😂🇮🇹 Stay until the last minutes — the new Belvedere reveal is worth it!

In this episode, EVERYTHING goes wrong:
• We drop our drinks straight into the Mediterranean 🌊
• A window nearly flies off the villa (seriously!) 💨
