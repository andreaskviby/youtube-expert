SICILY is AMAZING! But SNOW? (#8)

Published: May 18, 2025

We Had NO Idea It Would Be Like This! 😱❄️

We traveled to Lapland for New Year's Eve, expecting snow and beautiful scenery. What we didn't expect were the magical Northern Lights dancing across the sky and unforgettable encounters with reindeer up close.
This Arctic adventure completely surprised us and became one of the most memorable experiences of our lives. Join us as we explore the winter wonderland of Lapland and celebrate the New Year far from sunny Sicily.
