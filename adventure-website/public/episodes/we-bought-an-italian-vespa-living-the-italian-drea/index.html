We Bought an Italian Vespa | Living the Italian Dream in Sicily (#17)

Published: August 17, 2025

We Bought a Sicilian Vespa. Living the Italian Dream in Sicily. We did it! 🎉 In this episode, we’re celebrating an unforgettable moment in our Sicilian adventure — we bought our very own Sicilian Vespa! 🇮🇹✨

This isn’t just any scooter — it’s a classic Piaggio Vespa, full of history and charm, perfectly matching our dream of living the authentic Italian lifestyle. From birthday celebrations to cruising through the Sicilian countryside, this episode is filled with laughter, joy, and a true sense of community.

💛 In this video, you’ll see:
• Linda’s birthday celebration 🎂
• The big Vespa reveal 🛵
• How we found our dream scooter
• What makes owning a Vespa in Sicily so special
• Heartwarming moments with friends and family
• How our expat life in Italy keeps evolving

This Vespa isn’t just transportation — it’s a symbol of freedom, culture, and connection. It’s a piece of Italy that represents our journey of embracing life abroad, one adventure at a time.
