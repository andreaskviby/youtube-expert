From House Owners to Olive Farmers? 🫒 Our Next Sicilian Adventure (#42)

Published: December 28, 2025

🫒🇮🇹 We Just Made an Offer on Our Dream Olive Farm in Sicily!
Two years ago, we took a leap of faith and bought a house in Sicily.
Now, we're standing at the edge of an even bigger dream.
For a long time, Linda has dreamed of having her own piece of land—a place where we can grow food, harvest olives, make wine, cook outdoors, and create unforgettable experiences surrounded by nature.
After months of searching, we may have finally found it.
Nestled in a peaceful Sicilian valley lies a breathtaking 15,000-square-meter property that feels almost too good to be true.
🌿 Around 150 olive trees
🍇 A vineyard producing its own wine
🍊 Orange, lemon, fig, peach, walnut, and other fruit trees
💧 A massive 70,000-liter water reservoir with a complete irrigation system
🏔️ Stunning mountain views
🌊 And on clear days... even a glimpse of the sea
In this episode, we take you with us as we explore the property, harvest olives, visit the olive mill, press our own extra virgin olive oil, and imagine what the future could look like.
Could this become the next chapter of our Sicilian adventure?
We're dreaming about so much more than just owning land.
Imagine:
✨ Olive harvest weekends
✨ Farm-to-table dinners
✨ Wine tastings
✨ Outdoor cooking experiences
✨ Glamping under the Sicilian stars
✨ A place where friends, family, and guests can experience authentic Sicily
We've already made our offer.
Now comes the hardest part...
Waiting.
Will this incredible property become ours?
Join us as we take another step toward building the life we've always dreamed of in Sicily.
🤞 Fingers crossed.
