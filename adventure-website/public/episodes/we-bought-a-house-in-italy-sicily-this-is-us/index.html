We didn’t just buy a house — we bought an adventure! 🏡✨
We’re Linda & Andreas, a Swedish couple who traded routine for a dream life in Sicily, Italy. When our 6 kids left home, we decided to start fresh — renovating a 160sqm historic home, learning Italian, and embracing local culture one challenge at a time. 🇮🇹💪

Join us as we share the ups and downs of renovating in Sicily, exploring Italian food, village life, and living abroad after 50. From DIY projects to breathtaking views, this is our journey of transformation — both home and heart. ❤️
