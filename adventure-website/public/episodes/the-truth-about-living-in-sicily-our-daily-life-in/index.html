🏡🇮🇹 We Bought an Abandoned House in Sicily... and This Is Our New Everyday Life

When we moved from Sweden to a small town in Sicily, we dreamed of a simpler life.
Less stress.
More sunshine.
Fresh food.
And time to enjoy the little things.
In this episode, we're taking you along for a glimpse of what everyday life really looks like in our corner of Sicily.
Our day begins at the local Friday market, one of our favorite weekly traditions. The streets fill with colorful stalls, friendly conversations, and some of the freshest produce you can imagine.
🍊 Sun-ripened fruit picked just hours earlier
🦪 Fresh seafood from the Mediterranean
🧀 Local delicacies and traditional Sicilian flavors
The market isn't just a place to shop—it's where the community meets, catches up, and shares the rhythm of life that makes Sicily so special.
But living in a Sicilian hill town comes with its own surprises.
Forget the gym...
Here, daily life is the workout. 😅
Steep streets, endless staircases, and dramatic elevation changes mean every errand comes with breathtaking views—and a little extra cardio.
As we wander through the town, you'll experience the sights, sounds, flavors, and atmosphere that made us fall in love with Sicily in the first place.
No tourist attractions.
No travel guide highlights.
Just real life.
Slow mornings.
Fresh food.
Beautiful surroundings.
And the simple joy of living in one of the most fascinating places in the Mediterranean.
If you've ever dreamed of moving abroad, slowing down, or experiencing authentic Italian life beyond the tourist hotspots, this episode is for you.
