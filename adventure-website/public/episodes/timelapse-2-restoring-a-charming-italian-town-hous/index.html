Timelapse 2 | Restoring a Charming Italian Town House | Renovation in Sicily

Published: March 07, 2026

We bought an abandoned house in Sicily and this is our renovation vlog.
Follow our journey restoring a traditional Italian home in rural Sicily.

We’re bringing an old Sicilian town house back to life! 🇮🇹
In this timelapse renovation video you’ll see how several parts of the house transform step by step. We renovate a bedroom, restore and refresh a balcony, install new windows, and give old materials a second life by crafting a new door from an old one. You’ll also see window frames being painted and a staircase getting a fresh upgrade with color and care.
This project is all about preserving the character of the house while slowly turning it into a beautiful home again. Watching the transformation happen in timelapse really shows how much work – and love – goes into every detail.
If you enjoy renovation projects, old houses, and Mediterranean charm, follow along as we continue restoring this Sicilian town house.
