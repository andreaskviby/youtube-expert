Italian Terrace Repair & Cabinet Upcycling in Sicily (#28)

Published: October 20, 2025

We Fixed a Rainwater Disaster. Terrace Repair & Cabinet Upcycling in Sicily. Rainwater chaos on our Sicilian terrace is finally under control! 🌧️ In this episode, we show how we fixed a major rainwater disaster—blocked pipes, overflowing terrace, and unexpected water damage—while continuing our mission to restore our historic dream home in Sicily.

From installing copper upgrades to improving drainage and sealing key areas, we take you step by step through our rainproofing process. Plus, we upcycle an old cabinet into something beautiful and functional!

✨ Why Watch?
• Real expat renovation in a historic Sicilian home
• Step-by-step rainproofing & terrace waterproofing fixes
• Copper plumbing adjustments that make a big difference
• DIY upcycling ideas for old cabinets
• Authentic daily life in Sicily as expats

Every project brings us closer to a safer, stronger, and more beautiful home. Thanks for being part of the journey—your support truly means the world! 💛
