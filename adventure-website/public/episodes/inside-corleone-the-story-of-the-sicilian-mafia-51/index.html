Inside Corleone: The Story of the Sicilian Mafia (#51)

Published: April 02, 2026

🇮🇹 Corleone.
For many people, the name immediately brings one thing to mind:
The Mafia.
For decades, this small Sicilian town became synonymous with power, fear, violence, and the rise of Cosa Nostra. It was here that some of the most notorious Mafia bosses built their influence, leaving a legacy that would shape Sicily's history and capture the world's imagination through books, films, and headlines.
In this episode, we travel to Corleone to uncover the real story behind the myths.
We explore the town's complex history, learn about the rise of the Sicilian Mafia, and reflect on the devastating impact organized crime had on communities across Sicily.
But this is not just a story about darkness.
It is also a story about courage.
We pay tribute to anti-Mafia heroes Giovanni Falcone and Paolo Borsellino, whose determination and sacrifice helped challenge the culture of fear and silence. Their legacy continues to inspire Sicily today and reminds us that even the most powerful systems can be confronted by those brave enough to stand up for what is right.
Yet what surprised us most was discovering a completely different side of Corleone.
Beyond the headlines and history lies a region of extraordinary beauty.
Rolling hills.
Ancient traditions.
Warm hospitality.
And a slower, more authentic Sicily that many visitors never get to see.
During our stay, we checked into Il Bevaio di Corleone, a charming agriturismo surrounded by nature, where we spent the night in a cozy bungalow and enjoyed a wonderful Sicilian dinner under the stars.
It was the perfect reminder that Corleone today is not defined by its past.
It is a place of resilience, culture, breathtaking landscapes, and people determined to write a new chapter for their community.
Join us as we discover the real Corleone — beyond the stereotypes, beyond the movies, and beyond the Mafia.
This is Corleone today.
And you're invited to come along.
