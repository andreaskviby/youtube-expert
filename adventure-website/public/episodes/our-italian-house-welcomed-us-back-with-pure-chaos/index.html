Our Italian House Welcomed Us Back With Pure Chaos 🇮🇹😂 🫣 (#48)

Published: March 12, 2026

🏡🇮🇹 We Bought an Abandoned House in Sicily... and Somehow Ended Up in the Middle of a Carnival.
After weeks away, we're finally back in Sicily.
But before we even have time to unpack, Sicily has other plans.
Linda arrives in Termini Imerese and immediately finds herself in the middle of one of the island's most spectacular traditions: Carnevale Termitano, often considered the oldest Carnival in Sicily.
The streets explode with music, giant floats, costumes, laughter, and thousands of people celebrating a tradition that has lived on for generations.
But as always, real life has a way of interrupting the party...
😅 Linda takes a nasty fall shortly after arriving, ends up bruised and battered, and somehow manages to destroy her phone in the process.
✈️ Meanwhile, Andreas returns from a work trip to Germany and jumps straight back into renovation mode.
And that's when things get serious.
After experiencing several small electrical fires in our house over the past year, Andreas explains one of the biggest challenges of renovating old Sicilian homes: how do you install modern electrical systems without destroying historic walls, ceilings, and character?
⚡ You'll see practical solutions, renovation realities, and important lessons that every owner of an old house should know.
🎨 At the same time, Linda returns to one of her favorite projects—bringing creativity back by painting a brand-new mural in our stairwell, adding another personal touch to our ever-evolving Sicilian home.
This episode has a little bit of everything:
🎭 Carnival celebrations
🏡 Historic house renovation
⚡ Electrical challenges and safety
🎨 Art and creativity
😅 Travel mishaps
❤️ Everyday life in Sicily
Because that's what life here really looks like.
One moment you're dancing at a 150-year-old Carnival.
The next you're discussing electrical fires and painting walls.
And honestly...
We wouldn't want it any other way.
