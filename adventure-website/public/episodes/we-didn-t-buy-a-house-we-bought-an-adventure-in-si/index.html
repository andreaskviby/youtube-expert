We didn’t buy a house. We bought an adventure in Sicily, Italy 🇮🇹✨

Published: June 17, 2026

🇮🇹✨ What if you stopped waiting for “someday” and started living your dream today?

We’re Linda & Andreas, a Swedish couple who decided to trade routine for adventure and buy a house in Sicily. Since then, life has been filled with renovation projects, unexpected challenges, incredible food, new friendships, breathtaking sunsets, and plenty of surprises.

On our YouTube channel, We Bought an Adventure in Sicily, you’ll follow our real-life journey as we:
🏡 Renovate an old Sicilian townhouse
🍋 Explore authentic Sicilian life
🍝 Discover amazing local food
🚐 Travel around Sicily and beyond
😂 Share both the victories and the mistakes

If you dream of Italy, love travel, enjoy renovation projects, or simply need a little inspiration to chase your own adventure, then our channel is for you.
