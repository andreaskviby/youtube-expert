PALERMO Street Food Paradise! Flea Market Treasures + Mondello Beach Day | (#22)

Published: September 28, 2025

Join us for an incredible week in Palermo, Sicily! From the vibrant Sunday flea market at Piazza Marina to the crystal-clear waters of Mondello Beach, we're exploring the best of Palermo's food scene and hidden gems.

In this episode (Part 1 of our Palermo series), we discover:
🇮🇹 Piazza Marina's famous Sunday flea market - vintage treasures and local crafts
🍕 Authentic Sicilian street food and beachside pizza
🏖️ A perfect beach day at stunning Mondello
🍝 Linda's homemade Sicilian pasta using Swedish garden tomatoes
🍮 The BEST dessert we've EVER had (with cotton candy!)

From €10 wagyu bao buns to fresh seafood and tequila sunsets, this is Palermo like you've never seen it before!
