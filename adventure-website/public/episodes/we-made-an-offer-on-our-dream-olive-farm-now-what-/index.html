We Made an Offer on Our Dream Olive Farm... Now What? 🫒 (#45)

Published: January 18, 2026

We started the process of buying land in Sicily back in October. It's now January... and we STILL haven't signed anything. 😅

In this video, we're sharing the REAL process of buying property in Italy - the good, the bad, and the missing 1983 document that's holding everything up.

If you're dreaming of buying an olive grove, vineyard, or farmhouse in Sicily (or anywhere in Italy), watch this first. It might save you months of frustration and thousands of euros.

🫒 THE PROPERTY:
• 15,000 m² (3.7 acres) of land
• ~150 olive trees
• Vineyard
• 2 structures
• 70,000 liter water tank with natural source

🎓 KEY LESSONS:
1. NEVER sign the compromesso before ALL paperwork is verified
2. Budget way more time than you think
3. Get a good team (notaio, geometra, agent)
4. Understand exactly how taxes are calculated
5. Patience is not optional in Italy
6. Be emotionally prepared to walk away
