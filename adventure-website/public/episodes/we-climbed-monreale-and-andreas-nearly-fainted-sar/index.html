We Climbed Monreale… and Andreas Nearly Fainted! 😂 | Sarti Spritz Ending (#35)

Published: November 16, 2025

Our Sicilian adventure continues — and this time we took on the legendary Monreale Monastery overlooking Palermo! 🇮🇹🌄 What started as a beautiful cultural visit quickly turned into an unforgettable (and hilarious!) moment… because Andreas massively underestimated his fear of heights. 😅

Join us as we climb higher and higher through the ancient monastery terraces, taking in the breathtaking views — while Andreas tries not to faint on camera 😂. His wife, calm as always, guides herself along as they make it all the way to the top (because of course they do — she always go ALL the way up!).

And after surviving Monreale’s epic heights?
We ended the day the only proper way you should in Sicily: Sarti Spritz with our amazing friends Marina and Patrik. 🍹✨ Good vibes, great laughs, and memories we’ll never forget.

If you love travel stories, raw reactions, good food, and authentic moments from real people exploring the world — this episode is for you. ❤️
