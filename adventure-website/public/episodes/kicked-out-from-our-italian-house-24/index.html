Kicked out from our Italian house (#24)

Published: October 05, 2025

We Got Kicked Out... So We Found Paradise in Sicily 🇮🇹

In this episode, we find ourselves kicked out of our house (don’t worry, it’s all in good) and heading out to explore the stunning little gem Sampieri and rain-soaked city of Noto in Sicily. Join us as we embrace the unexpected, soak in breathtaking architecture, and share a slice of our Italian lifestyle! We explore beaches, nearby cities and food.

🏛️ Why watch?
- Marvel at Noto’s incredible baroque architecture, including its famous enormous staircase.
- Experience the charm of Sicily, even in heavy rain, as we navigate the city's historic beauty.
- Get a taste of our expat life and the joys of spontaneous travel adventures.
- See how we balance remote work, family life, and our dream of exploring Italy.
- Explore the most amazing AirBnB in Sampieri by Antonino

🌧️ Despite the pouring rain, we couldn’t help but fall in love with Noto’s cultural treasures. Every corner we turned unveiled something amazing, proving that even bad weather can’t dampen the magic of this iconic Sicilian city. From wandering the streets to enjoying a unique rabbit dish, this day was full of surprises and authentic moments.

The beaches and view of Sampieri is nothing but amazing, we both fell in love with little fishermens town 20 meters from the sea.
