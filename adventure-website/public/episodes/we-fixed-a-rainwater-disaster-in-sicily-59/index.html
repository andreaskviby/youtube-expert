We Fixed a Rainwater DISASTER in Sicily 🇮🇹💧 (#59)

Published: June 08, 2026

Our Sicilian townhouse was flooding every time it rained! Water was coming down the terrace and into our home. We had to fix it fast before serious damage occurred.

In this episode, we show you how we solved our rainwater drainage problem with a simple copper pipe solution. What could have been a €5000+ disaster was fixed for under €500!

🏠 This is our renovation journey in Sicily, Italy where we bought an old townhouse and are transforming it into our dream Mediterranean home.
