Olive Harvest in Sicily With Friends | Ancient Grove Tour with 90-Year-Old Farmer (#30)

Published: October 26, 2025

Olive Harvest in Sicily With Friends. Ancient Grove Tour with 80-Year-Old Farmer. Experience the magic of the Sicilian olive harvest as we pick olives with friends, share laughs, and enjoy the warmth of Mediterranean life. ☀️

In this episode, we visit one of Sicily’s most breathtaking ancient olive groves, cared for by an inspiring 80-year-old farmer who has dedicated his life to growing and pressing the finest extra virgin olive oil. His wisdom, passion, and stories bring the grove to life — and he even shares his secrets to cultivating healthy trees and harvesting at the perfect moment.

If you love slow travel, local traditions, sustainability, farming culture, and authentic Italian experiences, this episode is for you.
