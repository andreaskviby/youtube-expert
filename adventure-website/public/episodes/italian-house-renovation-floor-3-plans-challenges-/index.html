Italian House Renovation: Floor 3 Plans & Challenges (#14)

Published: July 04, 2025

Join us as we plan the restoration of floor 3 in our 100 year old Italian home 🏛️✨

This episode takes you through the challenges of a broken ceiling, wobbly floor, rusted nails, and our dream to transform it into a beautiful bedroom with its own bathroom.

We discuss:
✅ The structural problems with the ceiling
✅ How salty Sicilian air rusts the nails
✅ The plan to remove and recycle old handmade tiles
✅ Ideas for adding a new bathroom for guests
✅ Our big vision to host friends and family in
