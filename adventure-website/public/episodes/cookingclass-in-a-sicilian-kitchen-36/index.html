Cookingclass in a Sicilian kitchen🍷 (#36)

Published: November 20, 2025

Cooking Sicilian Pasta, Wine & Chaos! 🍷 Join us in this vibrant episode as we dive into the heart of Sicily, bringing you a slice of Italian lifestyle infused with warmth, humor, and a little chaos. In this impromptu cooking session, we whip up a delicious pasta dish using fresh, local ingredients like zucchini, garlic, Salsiccia meat, and our very own cold-pressed olive oil.

Here’s what makes this episode special:
- 🍝 Traditional Sicilian cooking with a personal twist
- 🍷 Exploring wine culture and pairing it with homemade meals
- 🧀 Secrets to making your pasta unforgettable (hint: it’s all in the sauce!)
- 🌟 Real moments of laughter, spontaneity, and expat life in Sicily

This isn’t just about food—it’s about embracing the joy of living in the moment, sharing meals with loved ones, and celebrating the unique charm of Sicily. Our dream of a historic renovation and remote work here in this beautiful land comes alive in every episode.

Why watch?
- Discover authentic Sicilian cooking tips
- Get a taste of expat life and the challenges of living abroad
- Experience the beauty, quirks, and flavors of Italy
