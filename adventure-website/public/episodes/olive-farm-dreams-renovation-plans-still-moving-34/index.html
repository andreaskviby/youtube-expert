Olive Farm Dreams & Renovation Plans Still Moving (#34)

Published: November 09, 2025

Sick Days in Sicily. Olive Farm Dreams & Renovation Plans Still Moving. In this episode of “Dreaming Big: Olive Farms & Renovation Plans”, we share heartfelt updates from our life as expats in beautiful Sicily. Even while recovering from illness, our plans for olive farms, home restoration, and future projects remain in motion.

🌿 What’s Inside This Episode:
• Exploring our dream of owning an olive farm in Sicily
• Builders assessing the third-floor roof renovation
• Upcoming electrical rewiring updates for home certification
• Linda’s creative painting projects (once she’s better!)
• Preparing our Sicilian home for holiday visits
• Travel plans back to Sweden for Christmas & New Year
• Life as expats balancing remote work, renovations, and travel

Join us as we continue shaping our dream life in Italy—one renovation, roadmap, and recovery day at a time. 💛
