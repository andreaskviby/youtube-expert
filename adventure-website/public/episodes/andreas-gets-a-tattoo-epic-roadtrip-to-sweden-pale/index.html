Andreas Gets a Tattoo | Epic Roadtrip to Sweden! (Palermo → Genova Ferry Adventure) (#39)

Published: December 01, 2025

🇮🇹➡️🇸🇪 The journey home begins!
In this episode, Andreas starts the day by getting some fresh ink before we leave Sicily behind and begin our long road trip back to Sweden for Christmas.
With the tattoo finished and the holiday spirit in full swing, it's time for one last Sicilian tradition before departure: Glögg, packing, last-minute preparations, and saying goodbye to our beloved home in Sicily.
Then the real adventure begins.
We load the car, gather our olive oil, wine, Christmas gifts, and of course our brave travel companion 🐾, and make our way to the port of Palermo to board the ferry to Genova.
Join us as we take you behind the scenes of life on the road:
⛴️ Life aboard the ferry
🍽️ A huge lunch with Mediterranean views
🎄 Christmas preparations far from home
🚗 The first stage of our epic journey across Europe
🐱 How our cat handled the adventure
This episode is all about transitions — leaving one home behind while heading towards another. It's a mix of excitement, nostalgia, travel chaos, and the anticipation of spending Christmas with family in Sweden.
If you've ever wondered what it's like to live between two countries, travel thousands of kilometers with a car full of belongings, or simply enjoy following our Sicilian adventure, you'll love this episode.
And don't worry... this is only the beginning.
The next episode includes even more road trip adventures, unexpected moments, and the final stretch of our journey north.
