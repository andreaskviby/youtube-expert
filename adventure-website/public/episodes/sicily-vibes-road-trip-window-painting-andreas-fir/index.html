Sicily Vibes 🌿 Road-trip, Window painting & Andreas’ First Sicilian Tattoo! 🇮🇹 (#38)

Published: November 28, 2025

🇮🇹 Sometimes the best adventures are the ones you never plan...
Join us for another wonderfully chaotic day in Sicily as we set off on a mini-adventure filled with surprises, laughter, and a few moments that only seem to happen in Italy.
Our journey begins in the beautiful countryside around Ventimiglia di Sicilia, where breathtaking landscapes, winding mountain roads, and peaceful nature remind us why we fell in love with this island in the first place. But as always, Sicily has a few surprises waiting for us along the way...
🐑 Sheep suddenly appear and take over the road.
🐄 We investigate whether cows have hidden musical talents.
🌿 We soak in some of the most magical scenery Sicily has to offer.
🖌️ Back at home, the renovation continues as we tackle another painting project.
🎣 A small upgrade turns into another chapter of our never-ending house adventure.
🧑‍🎨 And to finish the day, Andreas makes a permanent decision and gets a brand-new tattoo!
This episode is a perfect snapshot of our life in Sicily: part travel adventure, part renovation project, part comedy show, and completely unpredictable.
If you're dreaming of life in Italy, love discovering hidden corners of Sicily, or simply enjoy following real people living an authentic and sometimes chaotic Mediterranean life, this episode is for you.
No luxury resorts.
No perfect plans.
Just real life, beautiful scenery, unexpected encounters, and another unforgettable day on our Sicilian adventure.
