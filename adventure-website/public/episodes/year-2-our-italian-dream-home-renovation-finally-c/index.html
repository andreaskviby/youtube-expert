Year 2 | Our Italian Dream Home Renovation Finally Comes Together (#19)

Published: September 02, 2025

Year 2 in Sicily. Our Dream Home Renovation Finally Comes Together. After two years of hard work, love, and creativity — our Sicilian home renovation is finally coming together! 🇮🇹✨

In this episode, we take you inside our historic home as we reveal the transformation that’s been years in the making. From hand-painted doors and restored walls to a refreshed façade and cozy details, every corner now tells a story of passion, patience, and the dream of living in Italy.

🏡 What You’ll See:

🎨 Beautiful freehand paintings on doors, balconies & hallways
🌿 Restored gardens and walls that bring warmth to the home
☀️ Exterior makeover giving the house a brand-new glow
🐾 Pet-friendly improvements and small details that make a big difference

This journey isn’t just about design—it’s about embracing the Italian lifestyle, balancing expat life, and finding joy in every step of restoration. If you love home makeovers, Italian culture, and slow living, this video will inspire your next dream project.
