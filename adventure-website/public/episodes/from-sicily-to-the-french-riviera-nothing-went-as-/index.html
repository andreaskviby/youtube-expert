✈️🇮🇹🇫🇷 We Thought We Were Going Home to Relax... We Couldn't Have Been More Wrong.

The plan sounded simple.
Leave Sicily.
Travel back to Sweden.
Take a few days off.
Relax.
Instead...
We somehow ended up dragging our entire lives across Europe.
😅
What started as a straightforward trip quickly turned into another adventure filled with missed expectations, heavy suitcases, long travel days, endless walking, spontaneous detours, and moments where we genuinely wondered what we were doing.
Along the way, we found ourselves exploring some of the most beautiful places on the Mediterranean coast.
🌴 The glamour of the French Riviera
⚓ The stunning peninsula of Saint-Jean-Cap-Ferrat
🇮🇹 The colorful streets of Sanremo
🍝 Incredible food
🌊 Spectacular sea views
🍷 Moments that reminded us why we love this lifestyle
Of course, no trip would be complete without a little chaos.
Or a lot.
There were train stations, airports, travel stress, aching feet, overloaded luggage, forgotten items, too much pasta, too little sleep, and at least one moment of...
"Wait... where are the passports?!"
😂
But that's life for us these days.
Living between Sweden and Sicily means that every journey becomes part travel documentary, part comedy show, and part survival challenge.
And honestly?
We wouldn't have it any other way.
❤️ Join us as we travel across Italy and France, chase beautiful views, enjoy incredible food, and somehow manage to survive another unforgettable adventure.
Because sometimes the best stories begin when your plans completely fall apart.
