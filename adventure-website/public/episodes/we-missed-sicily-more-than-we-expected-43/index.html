We Missed Sicily More Than We Expected 🇮🇹❤️ (#43)

Published: January 04, 2026

🎄🇸🇪🇮🇹 From a Swedish Winter Wonderland Back to Our Sicilian Dream
After spending a magical Christmas and New Year in Sweden surrounded by family, friends, snow, and traditions, it's finally time to begin the next chapter of our adventure.
For a few precious weeks, we exchanged Sicilian sunshine for snowy landscapes, cozy evenings, traditional Swedish Christmas celebrations, and quality time with the people we love most. We decorated the Christmas tree, enjoyed festive buffets, introduced our Italian friends to Swedish holiday traditions, and welcomed the New Year with laughter, food, and unforgettable memories.
But while Sweden gave us a beautiful holiday season, our hearts never stopped thinking about Sicily.
We miss the sunshine.
We miss the sea.
We miss the sound of life in Termini Imerese.
We miss our morning walks along the beach.
And we miss Belvedere, the place that has become our second home.
As 2025 begins, we're ready to return to Sicily and continue the journey that started with a dream and a house in a small Sicilian town.
In this episode, we look back on our Swedish holiday celebrations and share why we're so excited to head back south for another year filled with renovations, adventures, surprises, and life in beautiful Sicily.
