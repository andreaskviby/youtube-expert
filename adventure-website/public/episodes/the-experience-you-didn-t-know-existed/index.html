The experience you didn't know existed

Published: February 18, 2026

Casa Solivio offers you a spot in our olive grove in Sicily.
At Casa Solivio, we've reimagined luxury accommodation. Our safari tents combine the romance of sleeping under the stars with five-star comfort and amenities. Each tent is thoughtfully positioned to offer privacy, stunning views, and a deep connection to the surrounding olive groves.
Wake to birdsong and mountain breezes, enjoy farm-to-table breakfasts on your private terrace, and fall asleep to the gentle rustle of ancient olive trees. This is glamping as it should be - elegant, intimate, and unforgettable.
