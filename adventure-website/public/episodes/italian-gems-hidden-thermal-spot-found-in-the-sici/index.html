Italian Gems |Hidden Thermal Spot Found in the Sicilian mountains (#33)

Published: November 06, 2025

Secret places in Sicily
Fisherman Invades Our Kitchen. Hidden Thermal Spot & Augustali Vineyard Tour in Sicily. Experience one of our most unforgettable Sicilian weeks yet! 🇮🇹✨ It begins with a surprise visit from our neighbor — a fisherman who walked straight into our kitchen and cooked the freshest Mediterranean catch you can imagine! The flavors, the chaos, the laughter… pure Sicilian magic.

After the feast, we stumbled upon a hidden natural thermal spring in the mountains near Termini Imerese — a true local secret far from tourist routes. And to complete the adventure, we toured the beautiful Augustali Vineyard in Partinico, tasting incredible organic wines and learning about sustainable Sicilian winemaking.

📍 In This Episode:
• Fresh Mediterranean fish cooked by a local fisherman
• A secret mountain thermal spot near Termini Imerese
• Augustali Vineyard tour & organic wine tasting
• Authentic Sicilian lifestyle, food, and hidden gems

If you love Italian food experiences, small-town adventures, and off-the-map treasures, this vlog is for you.
