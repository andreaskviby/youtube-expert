This Could Be Your Life in Sicily… Would You Dare? 🇮🇹 (# 55)

Published: April 30, 2026

What started as a dream turned into three years of dust, chaos… and something unforgettable.
Two Swedes. One house in Sicily. No turning back.

Along the way, we discovered something surprising — there are thousands of abandoned and affordable homes all over this island.
Whether you dream of a full renovation project… or a beautiful, move-in-ready home — Sicily has it all.
Quiet village life where everyone knows your name…
or vibrant cities like Cefalù, Catania, and Palermo.
Houses are everywhere.
It’s not about finding one — it’s about finding the one.
(We visited one. Just one. And that was it… somehow it worked out.)
🌅 And this… this could be your life too.
✨ And if you don’t feel like searching?
We might have something for you.
A friend of ours is now selling their newly renovated apartment in Termini Imerese —
around 100 sqm, space for 5 people, two bathrooms (one with shower), stunning views, and incredibly tasteful design.
A true hidden gem.
🔥 And today?
Back in Sweden we would celebrate Walpurgis Night — gathering around bonfires, singing, and welcoming spring.
But here in Sicily… we’re lighting the grill instead.
Different tradition. Same feeling.
A new life, a new rhythm… and maybe a new dream.
👇 So tell us — could you see yourself living like this?
