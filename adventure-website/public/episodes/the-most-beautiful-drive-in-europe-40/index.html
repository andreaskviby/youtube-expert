🚙🇮🇹🇦🇹 From Sicily to Sweden – One Incredible View at a Time
Our journey across Europe continues, and today we're leaving Italy behind as we head north toward Austria on one of the most spectacular drives we've ever experienced.
The route along the A22 motorway takes us through breathtaking mountain landscapes, winding valleys, and views that seem almost too beautiful to be real. Every turn reveals another postcard-perfect scene, reminding us why we love traveling by car and exploring Europe at our own pace.
But not everything goes according to plan...
After spending the night in a historic castle, we wake up feeling less like royalty and more like we've been sleeping on medieval torture devices! 😅 With aching backs and slightly tired bodies, we hit the road once again, determined to continue our adventure.
Along the way, we share the reality of life on a long-distance road trip:
🚗 Endless kilometers across Europe
🏰 Unexpected castle stays
🏔️ Jaw-dropping Alpine scenery
☕ Much-needed coffee breaks
😄 Travel mishaps and plenty of laughter

This journey isn't just about getting from Sicily to Sweden. It's about embracing every moment along the way—the good, the uncomfortable, and the unforgettable.
If you've ever dreamed of driving through Europe, exploring hidden places, and experiencing life beyond the typical tourist routes, this episode is for you.
Come along as we continue our epic road trip north and discover why sometimes the journey becomes the destination.
