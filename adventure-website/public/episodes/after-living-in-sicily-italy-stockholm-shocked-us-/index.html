After Living in Sicily, Italy … Stockholm Shocked Us (#58)

Published: June 04, 2026

After spending months in Sicily, we returned to Stockholm for a day—and something felt different.
Living between Sweden and Sicily has changed the way we see both places. In this episode, we explore Stockholm with fresh eyes, enjoy some of the things we've missed, and reflect on what it's really like to call two countries home.
Can you ever truly belong to two places at once?
Join us as we navigate life between the north and the south, between two cultures, two lifestyles, and two very different versions of home.
