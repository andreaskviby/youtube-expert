🏡🇮🇹 We Bought a Sicilian Palazzino... and We're Bringing It Back to Life
Two years ago, we took a leap of faith and bought a historic Sicilian palazzino in the heart of a small town on the island.
A palazzino is a small palace-like residence — elegant, full of character, and rich in architectural details. While more modest than a grand palazzo, it still carries the charm, craftsmanship, and beauty of another era.
When we first stepped inside, we immediately fell in love with the painted ceilings, original floors, historic details, and endless potential hidden behind years of neglect.
Since then, we've spent the last two years restoring, renovating, learning, and dreaming.
And we're far from finished.
In this second house tour, we're taking you through the second and third floors of Casa Solivio to show you exactly how far we've come—and how much work still lies ahead.
🏡 What has been completed?
🎨 Which rooms have been transformed?
🔨 What challenges are still waiting for us?
✨ And what are our plans for the future?
From restored rooms and creative solutions to unfinished spaces full of possibility, this tour gives you an honest look at what it's really like to renovate a historic home in Sicily.
Whether you're dreaming of buying a house in Italy, restoring an old property, or simply love beautiful historic homes, we hope you'll enjoy this behind-the-scenes look at our ongoing adventure.
