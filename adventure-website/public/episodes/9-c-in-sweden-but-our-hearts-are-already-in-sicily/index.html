❄️🇸🇪 While Sweden Freezes, We Dream of Sicily ☀️🇮🇹
Winter has truly arrived in Sweden.
With temperatures dropping to -9°C, frozen landscapes, and snow-covered gardens, we're enjoying everything that makes a Scandinavian winter magical. Cozy family moments, outdoor cooking, crackling fires, and time spent with loved ones remind us why we will always cherish our Swedish roots.
But while we're embracing the cold, our thoughts keep drifting south...
To Sicily.
To sunshine, olive trees, mountain views, and the life we've built in our little corner of Italy.
As we prepare for our return, we're also looking ahead to what could be the most exciting year of our Sicilian adventure so far. New renovation projects are waiting, plans are taking shape, and our dream of owning an olive grove feels closer than ever.
In this episode, we slow down and enjoy the simple pleasures of winter while sharing what's happening behind the scenes and what's waiting for us when we return to Sicily.
🌲 A snowy Swedish winter
🔥 Family time around the fire
🍡 Marshmallow barbecue in freezing temperatures
🏡 Updates on our historic Sicilian home
🫒 The latest news about our olive grove dream
🇮🇹 Preparations for our return to Sicily
✨ Big plans for the year ahead
Living between two countries is a strange and beautiful experience. One moment we're surrounded by snow and winter darkness, the next we're dreaming of Mediterranean sunshine and life among the olive trees.
If you've ever dreamed of moving abroad, creating a new life in another country, or simply following your heart toward something bigger, we think you'll enjoy this episode.
The adventure is far from over.
In fact, it feels like it's only just beginning.
