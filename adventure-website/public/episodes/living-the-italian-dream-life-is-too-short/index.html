LIVING THE ITALIAN DREAM ... Life is too short ☀️

Published: February 22, 2026

Welcome to Casa Solivio — our brand new project in the olive groves! 🌿☀️
In this video, we’ll show you what the place looks like right now, the vision we’re building, and how you can be part of it.
🌳 Adopt an Olive Tree
Have you ever dreamed of having your own olive tree in the sun? With Casa Solivio, you’ll be able to adopt an olive tree and follow its journey through the season — from growth to harvest.
As an adopter, you’ll become part of the Casa Solivio story and feel closer to the land, the trees, and the process behind every olive.
🫒 Harvest experiences and possible glamping
We’re also planning experiences connected to harvest time — and we’re exploring the idea of offering glamping during the harvest season. Imagine peaceful evenings in the olive grove, hands-on harvest days, and a stay that lets you experience it all for real.
