Published: August 28, 2025

A Year in Sicily. Our Life-Changing Italian Adventure & Renovation Journey. We did it! 🇮🇹✨ Our first year in Sicily is complete — and what a life-changing adventure it’s been! From taking the leap to move abroad, embracing the Italian lifestyle, and tackling a historic home renovation, this episode shares the highs, lows, and everything in between.

This is more than just a travel story — it’s about courage, discovery, and creating a dream life in Italy. 🌿

💛 In this video, you’ll see:
• Why we chose Sicily as our new home
• What we accomplished in our first year abroad
• Behind-the-scenes of expat life and home renovation
• The joys and challenges of starting over
• Our reflections, lessons, and hopes for year two

If you’ve ever dreamed of moving to Italy, starting fresh, or living a slower, more meaningful life — this one’s for you.
