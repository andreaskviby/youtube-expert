LOVE OR HATE? Palermo Is a Test 🇮🇹👍🏻👎🏻

Published: February 16, 2026

Palermo is Sicily’s ultimate dividing city — a place many people complain about first, and fall in love with later. We begin in the famous chaos of Palermo traffic, then let the city reveal its other side: Teatro Massimo, cathedrals, and stunning architecture hiding behind the noise. The streets are a show in themselves — including a surprise opera performance in a piazza.
