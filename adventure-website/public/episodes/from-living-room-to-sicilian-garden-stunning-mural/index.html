From Living Room to Sicilian Garden | Stunning Mural Transformation (#5)

Published: April 27, 2025

BIG REVEAL, From Living Room to Sicilian Garden. Stunning Mural Transformation. We turned our living room into a Sicilian garden! 🌴🎨
In this episode, we reveal the incredible mural transformation that brought the countryside of Sicily right into our home. Forget plain white walls — we created a space filled with color, warmth, and life inspired by nature and our surroundings in Italy.

You’ll see:
🌿 How we designed and painted our Sicilian garden mural.
🎨 The creative process behind blending art and renovation.
🏡 How we’re turning our historic Sicilian house into a dream home.
🔥 Fires in Sicily — what we saw from our terrace and how we’re staying safe.

This journey is more than just a renovation—it’s about embracing the Italian lifestyle, connecting with creativity, and turning every space into something meaningful.
