We Opened the Boxes... And Couldn't Believe It ❤️📦 (#41)

Published: December 21, 2025

📖 After years of writing, dreaming, editing, doubting, rewriting, and believing... the day finally arrived.
Linda's new psychological thriller, IMAGO, has officially been released.
When the first boxes of freshly printed books arrived at our house, everything suddenly became real. What had lived for years as an idea, then a manuscript, and finally a finished novel, was now something we could hold in our hands.

This episode takes you behind the scenes of one of the biggest milestones of our lives.
You'll follow us through the emotional days leading up to the launch as we prepare handcrafted gift boxes for guests, decorate with olive branches from Sicily, bake traditional butterfly-shaped pastries, and put the finishing touches on a celebration that has been years in the making.
But this isn't just a story about publishing a book.
It's a story about courage.
About following a dream despite uncertainty.
About creating something from nothing and sharing it with the world.
You'll join us at the release party in Katrineholm, where family, friends, readers, and supporters gather to celebrate IMAGO's arrival. There are speeches, laughter, emotions, and moments we'll never forget.
❤️ In this episode:
📦 The arrival of the very first IMAGO books
🎁 Handcrafted launch gifts inspired by Sicily
🦋 Traditional butterfly pastries baked for the celebration
🎉 Behind the scenes of the release party
🗣️ Speeches from Andreas and Linda
📖 The story behind IMAGO and what inspired it
❤️ Family, friends, and readers celebrating together
IMAGO is a psychological thriller that explores identity, relationships, perception, and transformation. Parts of the story are inspired by the landscapes, atmosphere, and mysteries of Sicily — a place that has become an important part of our lives.
Whether you're a reader, a writer, a dreamer, or simply someone who loves seeing people pursue their passion, we hope this episode inspires you.
Because sometimes the biggest adventures don't begin with a plane ticket.
Sometimes they begin with a blank page.
📚 IMAGO is available now.
Thank you for being part of this journey.
