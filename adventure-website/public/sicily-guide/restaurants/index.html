Tips for Eating in Sicily

  • Lunch is the main meal - Restaurants serve their best food at lunch, and it's often cheaper
  • Look for "menu del giorno" - The daily menu is always fresh and good value
  • Avoid tourist menus - If it has photos, keep walking
  • Ask for local wine - "Vino della casa" is usually excellent and cheap
  • Riposo is real - Most places close 3-7pm, plan accordingly
