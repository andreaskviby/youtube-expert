The Garden of Eden, Palermo

8 / 10

The moment we stepped through the door, we were transported. What waited on the other side was a magical garden — soft, dreamy lighting, gentle music drifting through the greenery, and an atmosphere that genuinely took our breath away. It's a true oasis in the middle of the city: leave the busy streets of Palermo behind, walk in, and suddenly you're somewhere else entirely. The lighting, the music, and the food were all in perfect harmony — everything worked together as one experience rather than just a meal.

The food matched the setting beautifully, and the highlight for us was the dessert: Andreas was treated to an ice-cream tasting menu, and he and Linda happily worked their way through every scoop. It's the kind of place that turns dinner into a memory. If you're in Palermo, this is one you simply have to experience for yourself.
