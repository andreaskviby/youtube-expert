Types of Accommodation in Sicily

  • Agriturismo - Farm stays with homemade food, usually the best value
  • B&B - Small, family-run, personal service
  • Albergo - Traditional Italian hotels
  • Masseria - Converted farmhouses, often luxurious
  • Case Vacanze - Holiday apartments, great for longer stays

Booking Tips

  • Book direct when possible - Often cheaper and supports local owners
  • August prices spike - Book 3-4 months ahead for summer
  • Ask about breakfast - Sicilian breakfast is usually amazing
  • Check parking - Important if you're renting a car
