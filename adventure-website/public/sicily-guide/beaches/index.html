Beach Tips for Sicily

  • Go early or late - Beaches are empty before 10am and after 5pm
  • Bring water shoes - Many beaches are rocky, not sandy
  • August is crazy - All of Italy is on holiday, beaches are packed
  • Look for "spiaggia libera" - Free public beaches between the paid lidos
  • Pack a picnic - Some of the best beaches have no facilities
