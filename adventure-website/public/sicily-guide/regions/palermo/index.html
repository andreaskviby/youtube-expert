🏛️ Palermo & Northwest

Sicily's vibrant capital and beyond

Why Palermo?

Palermo is Sicily's chaotic, beautiful capital. Layers of history from Phoenicians, Greeks, Romans, Arabs, Normans, and Spanish have created a city unlike anywhere else. The street food is legendary - arancini, panelle, sfincione, and things you've never heard of.

The northwest coast offers some of Sicily's most beautiful beaches, the medieval town of Cefalù, and the unspoiled Madonie mountains.

Buying Property in Palermo

Palermo offers everything from €1 houses in need of total renovation to elegant apartments in restored palazzi. The historic center has seen major investment, and prices are rising but still affordable compared to northern Italy.

  • Historic center: From €20,000 (renovation) to €200,000+ (restored)
  • Cefalù area: Tourist premium, but stunning
  • Inland villages: Very affordable, authentic life

What to Know

  • Climate: Mediterranean, hot summers, mild winters
  • Transport: Palermo Airport (PMO), ferries to mainland
  • Character: Intense, chaotic, culturally rich, never boring
  • Challenges: Traffic, bureaucracy can be slow, noise in the center
