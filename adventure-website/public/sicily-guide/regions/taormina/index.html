🎭 Taormina & Messina

Sicily's glamorous corner

Why Taormina?

Taormina has attracted visitors since the Grand Tour - Goethe, D.H. Lawrence, and countless others fell in love with its dramatic position above the sea with Etna as a backdrop. The ancient Greek theatre with that view is unforgettable.

Beyond glamorous Taormina, the northeast offers the gateway to the Aeolian Islands (Lipari, Stromboli, Vulcano), the wild Nebrodi mountains, and authentic villages that tourism hasn't touched.

Buying Property in Taormina

Taormina itself is expensive by Sicilian standards - it's a luxury destination. But surrounding villages like Castelmola, Letojanni, and Giardini Naxos offer more accessible options while staying close to the beauty.

  • Taormina center: Premium prices, €200,000+ for apartments
  • Surrounding villages: More affordable, still beautiful
  • Aeolian Islands: Unique lifestyle, limited services
  • Nebrodi mountains: Very affordable, rural, authentic

What to Know

  • Climate: Mediterranean, cooler in the mountains
  • Transport: Catania Airport (1 hour), ferries to Aeolian Islands from Milazzo
  • Character: Touristic in Taormina, wild and authentic elsewhere
  • Challenges: Crowded in summer, Taormina prices, limited winter services on islands
