🏛️ Agrigento & Southwest

Ancient temples and authentic Sicily

Why Agrigento?

The Valley of the Temples is one of the most important archaeological sites outside Greece - stunning Greek temples in golden stone overlooking the sea. The Scala dei Turchi (Turkish Steps) is one of Sicily's most photographed beaches, with its white cliff formations.

The southwest is less developed for tourism than other parts of Sicily. This is authentic, rural Sicily - slower pace, lower prices, and a chance to experience the real island life.

Buying Property in Agrigento

This is where you'll find some of Sicily's best value properties. Several towns in the area have offered €1 houses to attract new residents. Even without those programs, prices are significantly lower than the east coast.

  • €1 house towns: Sambuca, Mussomeli, Bivona (with conditions)
  • Agrigento area: Affordable, close to the temples
  • Coastal properties: Still reasonable, beautiful beaches
  • Inland villages: Very low prices, authentic life

What to Know

  • Climate: Hot and dry, African influence, mild winters
  • Transport: Palermo or Catania airports (2+ hours), car essential
  • Character: Rural, authentic, less English spoken, traditional
  • Challenges: Distance from airports, limited services in small towns
