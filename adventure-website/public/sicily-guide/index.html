Living in Sicily: What We've Learned

When we first moved from Sweden to Sicily, we had no idea what to expect. The culture shock was real - from the way people shop at markets to the concept of "riposo" (afternoon rest). But over time, we've fallen deeply in love with this island and its people.

This guide will be our way of sharing what we've learned. Not the tourist version of Sicily, but the real one - the trattorias where locals actually eat, the beaches without the crowds, and the little hotels run by families who have lived here for generations.
