## YOUTUBE PARTNER PROGRAM (YPP)

### Eligibility Requirements (2024-2026)

#### Standard Monetization (Full YPP)
| Requirement | Threshold |
|-------------|-----------|
| Subscribers | 1,000 |
| Watch Hours (Long-form) | 4,000 hours in last 12 months |
| OR Shorts Views | 10 million Shorts views in last 90 days |
| Community Guidelines | No active strikes |
| 2-Step Verification | Required |
| AdSense Account | Linked and approved |

#### Early Access (Limited Monetization)
| Requirement | Threshold |
|-------------|-----------|
| Subscribers | 500 |
| Watch Hours | 3,000 hours in last 12 months |
| OR Shorts Views | 3 million Shorts views in last 90 days |
| Public Uploads | 3 in last 90 days |

### What Counts as "Eligible" Watch Hours

**DOES COUNT:**
- Public long-form videos (over 60 seconds)
- Views from the last 365 days (rolling)
- Embedded views (usually)
- Views from all countries

**DOES NOT COUNT:**
- Shorts (under 60 seconds) → separate Shorts monetization
- Private or unlisted videos
- Deleted videos
- Views older than 12 months
- Invalid/bot traffic (filtered by YouTube)
- Ad views (only organic content views)
- Views from videos that violate policies

### Why Hours Don't Match

Common reasons for discrepancy between "total hours" and "eligible hours":

1. **12-month rolling window** - Oldest hours "expire" daily
2. **Shorts excluded** - Go to separate Shorts fund
3. **Traffic quality filtering** - YouTube removes suspicious views
4. **Unlisted/Private** - Don't count even if watched
5. **Processing delay** - Can take 48-72 hours to update

## AD REVENUE BREAKDOWN

### Revenue Split
- **Creator:** 55% of ad revenue
- **YouTube:** 45% of ad revenue

### Shorts Revenue (from Feb 2023)
- Pooled revenue from Shorts Feed ads
- Split based on your share of total Shorts views
- Music licensing costs deducted first
- Typically $0.01-0.05 per 1000 views

### Long-form Revenue
- Typically $1-5 CPM (per 1000 views)
- Varies by:
  - Country of viewer
  - Content category
  - Time of year (Q4 highest)
  - Viewer demographics

## ANALYTICS METRICS EXPLAINED

### Watch Time vs Views
- **Views** = Number of times video started
- **Watch Time** = Total minutes/hours watched
- **Average View Duration** = Watch Time / Views

### CTR (Click-Through Rate)
- Impressions → Clicks
- **Good CTR:** 4-10%
- **Excellent CTR:** 10%+
- **Low CTR:** Under 2% (thumbnail/title issue)

### Retention
- **Average Percentage Viewed**
- **Good:** 40-60%
- **Excellent:** 60%+
- Key moments: First 30 seconds critical

### Traffic Sources
- Browse features (homepage)
- Suggested videos
- YouTube Search
- External (social media, websites)
- Shorts feed

## ALGORITHM FACTORS

### What YouTube Promotes
1. **High CTR** - People click on your thumbnail
2. **High Retention** - People watch most of video
3. **Session Time** - Viewers stay on YouTube after your video
4. **Engagement** - Likes, comments, shares, saves

### Video Length Recommendations
- **Shorts:** Under 60 seconds
- **Regular:** 8-15 minutes (sweet spot for ads)
- **Long-form:** 20-45 minutes (for dedicated audiences)

## RE-LAUNCH STRATEGIES

### Thumbnail A/B Testing
```
YouTube Studio → Content → Select Video →
Details → Thumbnail → Test & Compare
```
- Test up to 3 thumbnails
- YouTube auto-selects winner

### End Screens (Last 20 seconds)
- Link to other videos
- Subscribe button
- Best for boosting old content

### Cards (During video)
- Link to relevant videos at key moments
- Use when mentioning related content

### Playlists
- Group related videos
- Increases session time
- "Play All" drives sequential views

### Community Posts
- Re-share old videos
- Polls asking what to revisit
- Behind-the-scenes from old episodes

### Shorts → Long-form Pipeline
- Create 30-60 second clips from long videos
- Add CTA: "Full video in bio/comments"
- Drives traffic to main content

## COMMON ISSUES & FIXES

### "Not Eligible for Monetization"
1. Check 12-month watch hours specifically
2. Ensure channel is in good standing
3. Verify all requirements in Studio

### Low Watch Hours
- Focus on long-form content (8+ minutes)
- Improve retention (hook in first 30 sec)
- Create series/playlists

### Low CTR
- Better thumbnails (faces, emotions, text)
- Curiosity-gap titles
- Test with A/B feature

### Revenue Drops
- Seasonal (Q1 always lower)
- Check for yellow $ icons (limited ads)
- Review content for policy issues
